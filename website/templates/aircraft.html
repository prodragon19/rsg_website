About the VFW-Fokker 614

The VFW-Fokker 614 was a unique aircraft — the only jet-powered airliner ever built by the German-Dutch consortium VFW-Fokker. With its distinctive overwing engines, it remains one of the most unique aircraft from the 1970s.

Our addon brings this rare aircraft to Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024 with a high level of detail and authentic systems simulation.

Key Features
  • • Highly detailed 3D model
  • • Custom avionics and systems
  • • Realistic flight dynamics
  • • Authentic sound set
