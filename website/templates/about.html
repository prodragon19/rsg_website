Who are we?

The people behind RSG Software

We build realistic aircraft for serious sim pilots

RSG Software was founded by Eliaz and Jan, who both have big passions in the flight simming world. RSG Software was built to give the customers realistic, high fidelity aircrafts for a competing price

Our Mission

To develop the most realistic and well-crafted aircraft addons for Microsoft Flight Simulator. We believe that quality and attention to detail matter more than quantity.

Our Vision

To become one of the most respected developers in the flight simulation community by delivering products that sim pilots can trust and enjoy for years to come.

What we believe in

Quality First

We would rather release one excellent product than many average ones.

Realism & Accuracy

Every system is researched using real aircraft documentation and procedures.

Community Driven

We listen to our users and continuously improve our products based on feedback.
